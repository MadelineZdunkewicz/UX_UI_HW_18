My Skills

User Research

I collect qualitative and quantitative data to discover users’ needs and inform design decisions. I am skilled in persona creation, user interviews, usability testing, and web analytics.

Strategizing & Ideation

I advocate for users while prioritizing solutions that will best meet their needs. I am skilled in writing brand position and value proposition statements, as well as card sorting and site mapping to improve information architecture.

Storytelling

I enjoy studying target users to discover their thoughts and needs. After empathizing with them, I love telling their story through user scenarios, storyboards, and journey maps.
